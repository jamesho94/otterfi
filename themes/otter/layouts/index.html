Personal Finance 2.0

Our approach to personal finance revolves around the simple premise that managing your money like a business will improve your financial situation without radically changing your lifestyle.

This blog will provide you with a framework to objectively evaluate your finances and monitor changes over time. You'll learn how to apply corporate finance and accounting principles to optimize your spending, maximize your savings, and increase your personal profitability. Start managing your money like a business so that you can profit like one

Best of all, everything on this site is 100% free.
