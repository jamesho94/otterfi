Personal Finance 2.0

Our approach to personal finance revolves around the simple premise that managing your money like a business will improve your financial situation without radically changing your lifestyle.

This blog will provide you with a framework to objectively evaluate your finances and monitor changes over time. You'll learn how to apply corporate finance and accounting principles to optimize your spending, maximize your savings, and increase your personal profitability. We're here to help you manage your money like a business so that you can profit like one

Best of all, everything on this site is 100% free.

About Us

We’re professional accountants with a passion for personal finance (and otters)! We started this blog as a response to the prevalence of biased, misinformed, and just plain bad financial advice that we kept encountering online. Our promise to you is that everything you read on this site is backed by our research and is not influenced by any factor other than what we think will help you manage your financial life.

We hope that you’ll benefit from the information that we provide and if you have any questions, please don’t hesitate to reach out.
